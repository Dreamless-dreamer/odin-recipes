Finished Carrot Cake Pictured Above


How to make Carrot Cake Step-By-Step

  1. Make the batter: Beat the wet ingredients together, then mix in the dry ingredients. Stir in the carrots and fold in the pecans
  2. Bake the cake: Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan or pans. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick comes out clean.
  3. Frost the cake: Beat the frosting ingredients together until smooth and creamy. Stir in the pecans. Frost the (completely cooled) cake.

Ingredents Needed

  • Ingredients needed for carrot cake recipe:
  • White sugar for cake and confectioners’ sugar for frosting.
  • Neutral oil (vegetable oil) for moisture without flavor.
  • Four whole eggs for moisture and binding.
  • Two teaspoons of vanilla extract for cake and one teaspoon for frosting.
  • All-purpose flour for structure and binding.
  • Baking soda and baking powder as leaveners.
  • Ground cinnamon for warm, cozy flavor.
  • Salt for enhancing other ingredients’ flavors.
  • Grated carrots for the cake.
  • Chopped pecans for crunch in cake and frosting.
  • Butter and cream cheese for light and fluffy cream cheese frosting.
